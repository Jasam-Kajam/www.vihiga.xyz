Youth Empowerment Program Kicks Off in Western Kenya

By Editorial Desk • Oct 27, 2025

The county government has officially launched a youth empowerment program aimed at providing thousands of young people with digital skills and business training opportunities. The initiative seeks to tackle unemployment while promoting innovation and creativity among youth groups.

Governor Wafule emphasized that the training will be rolled out across all sub-counties and will include partnerships with local universities and technology firms to ensure practical, real-world experience for participants.

Participants will benefit from start-up funding, mentorship, and access to co-working spaces once they complete the program successfully. The initiative has already sparked enthusiasm from local communities and the private sector.
